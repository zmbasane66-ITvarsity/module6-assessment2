About Us

Our brand:

Zimi's Group is South Africa’s leading automotive group, employing over 700 people. We are a diversified (non-manufacturing) business in the automotive sector with unrivalled scale and scope in South Africa, a selected international presence primarily in the United Kingdom and Australia, as well as a limited presence in South East Asia and Southern and East Africa

Zimi's traces its roots back to 2000 when its founding company Imperial Holdings started as a humble motor dealership in downtown Johannesburg, Barney’s Super Service Station. Motus Holdings Limited (“Zimi's”) was listed on the JSE in November 2018, following its unbundling from Imperial Holdings Limited.
